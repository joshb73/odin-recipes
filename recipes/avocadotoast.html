Avocado Toast

Avocado toast is a deliciously hearty breakfast. If you are not vegan, you can serve fried eggs on t op for a delicious vegetarian meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 avocado, halved and pitted
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Toast bread in a toaster or toaster oven
  2. Mash avocado, parsley, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, black pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder together in bowl using a potato masher; spread on top of toast slices.
